About me

Hello there! I'm Andrei, a passionate photographer with a profound appreciation for the art of film. In an era dominated by digital technology, I find joy and inspiration in the timeless allure of film photography.

With every click of the shutter, my goal is to capture moments that transcend time, telling stories in a way only film can. The distinctive grain and rich tones of film allow me to craft images that exude nostalgia and authenticity.

Whether I'm spontaneously documenting life's candid moments or meticulously planning a photo series, I believe in the enchantment of analog photography. It enables me to convey emotions and atmospheres in a manner that digital mediums struggle to replicate.

Join me on this journey as we explore the world through the unique lens of film. Embracing the imperfections, each frame becomes a piece of art. I look forward to sharing my visual stories with you.
